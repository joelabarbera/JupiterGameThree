How to Play

You have $800 to spend from Monday to Friday. Each day, make choices about how to use your money. Each activity has a happiness score. Balance between budgeting and being happy.

Goal: Spend your money wisely!
To win, you must have spent all your money or have some left over by the end of the week and have the happiness bar at least 50%.

Watch out: If you end the week in debt, you lose the game.
