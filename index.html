Welcome to Budget Simulator

Knowing how to create a budget helps you stay out of debt,
whether big and small.

How to play

You have a job in NYC and have $700 to spend for the week (Monday to Friday). Each day, make decisions on how to spend your money.

To win the game, you must have spent all your money or have left over money by the end of the week.

To lose the game, you must be in debt by the end of the week.
