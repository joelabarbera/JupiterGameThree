Knowing how to create a budget helps you stay out of debt,

How to Play

You have $700 to spend from Monday to Friday. Each day, you'll make choices about how to use your money.

Goal: Spend your money wisely! To win, you must either spend all your money or have some left over by the end of the week.

Watch out: If you end the week in debt, you lose the game.
